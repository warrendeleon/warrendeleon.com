- **Cycle time**, not story points. How long from "picked up" to "in production"?
- **Deployment frequency**. Are we shipping daily or batching into risky fortnightly releases?
- **Time to recover**. When something breaks, how fast do we fix it?
- **Team sentiment**. Regular, honest check-ins. Not annual surveys.

What I deliberately don't track:

1. Lines of code
2. Number of PRs
3. Individual velocity
4. Hours worked
